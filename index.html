MAKE EVERY CITY REVOLVE
Around You



Have you ever lived in a new city and wished you had a friend who knows about you and the city well enough to recommend you places to go? How about meeting people with similar interest as you explore the city together?

With Xperio, you’ll no longer have to spend hours searching for places. We’ll recommend you various experiences or things to do that are tailored to your interest, available with just the tap of a finger. The more you use the app, the better the recommendations will be.

From restaurants to events to entertainment to iconic places, we've got it all personalized for you. By connecting you with people who share the same interest with you, you will definitely enjoy the city better, because what's a great experience without great people, right?





What makes us different?

Personalization

Using machine learning, we are able to learn about you over time and provide real-time personalized recommendations.

Various Experiences

We catagorize our expereinces into 5 different catogories. Food, entertainment, events, exploration and recreational activities.

Group Recommendations

Exploring the city with your partner or a group of friends? Xperio could provide recommendations based on the interest of everyone in a group.

MEET SIMILAR PEOPLE

Make friends who share the same interest as you. From the girl 2 blocks down who loves sushi to the boy across the street that enjoys the same adventurous activities as you do.
